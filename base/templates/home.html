About Us

CHRIST (Deemed to be University) campus is located in Lavasa a small hill station which is 60-70 kms away from the city of Pune.Due to this, it isn't easy to travel from Lavasa to Pune. But our esteemed institution offers a weekend bus service every week to Pune so the students can purchase or buy any required things. To optimize this we have created a web application so the students canbook the tickets every week.Chribus offers weekend bus services connecting Lavasa to Pune, making travel convenient for students and travelers alike. Explore our services and book your tickets today!
