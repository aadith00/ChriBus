ABOUT

CHRIST (Deemed to be University) was born out of the educational vision of St Kuriakose Elias Chavara, an educationalist and social reformer of the nineteenth century in South India.The campus is located in Lavasa a small hill-station which is 60-70 kms away from the city of Pune.Due to which it is quite difficult to travel from Lavasa to Pune.But our esteemed institution offers a weekend bus service every week to Pune so the students can purchase or buy any required things.To optimize this we have created a web application so the students are able to book the tickets every week.
